Selected Projects

FPV Payload Drone (IAM3D 2025)

Fully 3D‑printed FPV drone optimized for rapid fabrication and field repair.

Objective: Design a fully 3D‑printed FPV payload drone optimized for rapid fabrication, durability, and instant field repair.

Design: DFAM principles, print‑in‑place joints, generative geometry, PLA/TPU struct‑core elastomer frame, electromagnet payload device.

Analysis: Static displacement FEA (~10 N per motor) → ~7.9 mm peak deflection.

Results: >2:1 T/W, ~16 min endurance, ~4.9 kg payload, survived 13 impacts at 40–60 mph.

50 lb Combat Robot

High‑energy horizontal spinner engineered within NHRL safety limits.

Objective: Design a compact, high‑energy spinner weapon system.

Design: Single‑piece weapon bar + hub, optimized inertia, bearing‑supported shaft constraints.

Analysis: Von Mises stress FEA under centrifugal loading with refined hub mesh.

Results: ~25 kJ weapon energy, geometry validated for competition use.

FPV Rover Drivetrain (IAM3D 2026)

Modular tracked drivetrain designed for abrasive terrain.

Objective: Design a fully 3D‑printed tracked drivetrain for sandy terrain.

Design: Pin‑jointed modular track system, sprocket‑driven traction, printable components.

Analysis: Worst‑case static load approximations, bench validation.

Results: Smooth tracked motion, minimal derailment, modular repairability.
